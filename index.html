Instructions overview

Our repair kits consist of a customized high quality aftermarket Actuator (with lifetime warranty) which is easier and faster to install than OEM, plus you do not need to uninstall the OEM actuator, so just install our kit and you are done!( while keeping all original features!!)
The following steps are just an overview of the steps to install the actuator but you will receive a detailed set of step-by-step instructions specifiying everything from how to remove the door panel to how to install the actuator and wires. normally for first time the estimated time to install is about 1.5HR.
If you do not feel confortable working on your car, keep in mind that your own mechanic could do it for you and you'll still spend less money than paying the dealer $500 for part + labor. (thats what the dealer charges per door)

  • First you need to remove the door panel
  • Install the customized Actuator by installing two screws to hold it in place.
  • Drill a small hole to finish installing the actuator
  • Connect two wires from the actuator to the door (using the easy "t-tap" quick connectors included in the kit)
  • Re-install the door panel and you are done.


Required tools:

  • Phillips & Flat screw drivers
  • 10mm socket & Ratchet
  • Utility Knife
  • Pliers
  • Drill with a 7/64 drill bit (or equivalent)
  • OPTIONAL: Light/circuit electric tester
